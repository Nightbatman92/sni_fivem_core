### Our Pledge
As contributors, maintainers, and community members of **Skull Network Italia**, we are committed to maintaining a **professional, respectful, and collaborative** environment, free from harassment or toxic behavior.

This project represents the work and identity of the Skull Network Italia community.

---

### Expected Behavior
- Use respectful and inclusive language
- Accept constructive criticism
- Collaborate professionally
- Respect maintainer decisions
- Report issues appropriately

---

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive language
- Personal attacks or flame wars
- Spam, trolling, or sabotage
- Sharing private information
- Attempting to bypass security policies
- Abuse of issues, PRs, or communication channels

---

### Enforcement
Maintainers of **Skull Network Italia** reserve the right to:
- remove non-compliant content
- close issues or PRs
- suspend or ban contributors
- restrict repository access

Maintainer decisions are final.

---

### Reporting
Inappropriate behavior may be reported privately to the maintainers via the official Discord.
